Top three activities to do at Acapulco

Watch the Sunset at "la Quebrada"

One of the best sunsets you can see in your life, you will see it in "La Quebrada".

World's longest zipline over the sea

You can cross the Bay of Puerto Marques or demonstrate your courage and skill in Xmonkey, a unique rope park in the region.

Visit "Mi Postrería"

As is name can tell you, is a vibrant desserts store, where you can find a delightful breakfast, brunch or a dessert from their menu, wich includes Ice Cream Rolls, Pancakes, Waffles, You can also play board games while enjoying its varied menu. by far they have the best desserts of the city!
